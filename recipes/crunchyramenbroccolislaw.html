Crunchy Ramen Broccoli Slaw

  • 1 (16 ounce) package broccoli coleslaw mix 2 tablespoons dried minced onion
  • ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ cup salad oil 1 (4 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
